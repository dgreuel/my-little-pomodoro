Customizing Timers

The standard Pomodoro Technique uses a 25 minute "Pomodoro", a 3-5 minute short break, and a 15-30 minute long break. These timer lengths may not fit your needs, so My Little Pomodoro lets you change them.

To change the timer lengths:

  1. Open the application preferences.
    • choose My Little Pomodoro > Preferences...
    • or click the "Preferences" button in the toolbar
    • or type âŒ˜,
  2. Click on the "General" section.
  3. Change the timer lengths to something that better suits your working style.

Note: Any changes made to the length of the current timer will take effect when the timer is reset or when the timer finishes.

By default, each timer starts automatically once it is selected. This behavior can be changed.

To change if the timers start automatically:

  1. Follow steps 1 through 2 above.
  2. Check or uncheck "Automatically start timer when selected".

Note: The next time you select a timer, you will need to manually start it by clicking on the "Start" button.
