Instant Messenger Integration

If you use iChat, Adium, or Skype, My Little Pomodoro can set your status to away when you start a Pomodoro. You can also enter a custom status message.

To enable Instant Messenger integration:

  • Open the application preferences.
    • choose My Little Pomodoro > Preferences...
    • or click the "Preferences" button in the toolbar
    • or type âŒ˜,
  • Click on the "Messengers" section.
  • Click on the checkbox of each IM application you want My Little Pomodoro to integrate with.
  • You can optionally enter a custom status message. There are no variables currently supported, but if you request any, I will do my best to add them.

Note: Skype will prompt you to allow My Little Pomodoro access to Skype. Also, My Little Pomodoro will not set your mood message in Skype, and will only set your status to Do Not Disturb.

When the timer finishes or is reset, your status will be set back to available.
