Audio Alarms

When the timer finishes, a sound will play. In the "Audio" section of the application's Preferences, you can choose whether you would like the sound to play, and which sound you would like to use for the alarm. You are also given the choice of having the application speak to you when the timer is finished.

To change the audio alarms:

  1. Open the application preferences.
    • choose My Little Pomodoro > Preferences...

  2. Click on the "Audio" section.

Ticking Sounds

You might find it helpful to have a ticking sound playing while the timer is running. My Little Pomodoro provides you with the option to enable ticking sounds, and provides a few different ticking sounds for you to choose from.

To change the ticking sound preferences:

  1. Follow steps 1 through 2 above.
  2. Change the ticking sound preferences.
