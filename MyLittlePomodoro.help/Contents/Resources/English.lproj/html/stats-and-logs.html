Statistics and Logs

My Little Pomodoro keeps track of all of your tasks and each work unit (pomodoros, short breaks, and long breaks) that you've completed, and it makes this data available to you through the Statistics and Logs window.

To view the Statistics and Logs window:

  • Choose Tools > Statistics/Task Log/Work Unit Log
  • or type ⇧⌘S
  • or right-click on the My Little Pomodoro dock icon and choose Statistics and Logs...
  • or click on the menu bar icon (if enabled) and choose Statistics and Logs...
